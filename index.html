Research Focus

My research focuses on embodied AI, visual perception, reasoning, and robotic control. I investigate how embodied agents can develop environmental awareness through vision, including affordance understanding [P1, P2, p1, p2] and spatial reasoning [P3], to perform complex manipulation tasks. My current work leverages large multimodal foundation models to explore these challenges.
