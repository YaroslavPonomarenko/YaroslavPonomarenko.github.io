Iaroslav Ponomarenko is a second-year master’s student in Computer Science at Peking University’s Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies, where he works under the guidance of Prof. Hao Dong in the Hyperplane/AGIBot PKU Lab. His research focuses on embodied AI, visual perception, reasoning, and robotic control, with a particular emphasis on enabling agents to develop environmental awareness for complex manipulation tasks. Iaroslav has contributed to several key projects, including SpatialBot, a Vision-Language Action model for spatial reasoning, and ManipVQA, a framework that integrates robotics knowledge into multi-modal large language models. Before joining Peking University, Iaroslav completed his engineering studies in Russia and has gained professional experience through internships and research roles in AI and machine learning.
